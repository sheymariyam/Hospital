About us

About us

Founded in 1999, LifeBridge Hospital began as a single medical facility incolombo, with the mission to deliver high-quality, compassionate healthcare to our community. Over the years, we have grown into a leading healthcare provider with three branches, each equipped with state-of-the-art technology and a wide range of specialized services. From our humble beginnings, we have remained committed to excellence in patient care, innovation, and supporting the health and well-being of every individual we serve. Today, LifeBridge Hospital continues to uphold its founding values, providing advanced medical treatments and personalized care across all our locations.

Our Branches

LifeBridge Hospital is proud to serve our community through three modern branches, each designed to provide top-quality healthcare tailored to the specific needs of the local population. Across all locations, we maintain a commitment to medical excellence, cutting-edge technology, and compassionate care.

Colombo Branch

This flagship location is our largest and most comprehensive facility, equipped to handle everything from routine check-ups to advanced medical emergencies.

Specialties

Oncology: Comprehensive cancer care services including chemotherapy, radiation therapy, and immunotherapy. Our oncology center works closely with top research institutions to offer clinical trials.

Geriatrics: Specialized care for the elderly, focusing on chronic disease management, fall prevention, memory care, and palliative services.

Endocrinology: Advanced treatment for diabetes, thyroid disorders, and other hormonal imbalances.

Facilities and Services

  • Dedicated Oncology Unit with advanced chemotherapy suites
  • Inpatient Geriatric Care, focusing on holistic, long-term treatment
  • Outpatient services with same-day appointments
  • Cutting-edge diagnostics and laboratory services

Our Location

Kandy Branch

Our second branch combines cutting-edge technology with a patient-first approach, offering world-class care in key specialties.

Specialties

Cardiology: Featuring the latest in diagnostic imaging and interventional cardiology for heart disease treatment.

Neurology: State-of-the-art neuroimaging, stroke management, and neurological disorder treatments.

Facilities and Services

  • 24/7 Emergency Department
  • 24/7 Emergency Department
  • Comprehensive Inpatient Care with specialized ICUs
  • Full-service Radiology and Imaging Department
  • Multidisciplinary outpatient clinics offering follow-up and rehabilitation

Our Location

Nugegoda Branch

Our third location specializes in surgical excellence and women’s health, providing a compassionate and family-friendly environment for patients.

Specialties

General and Minimally Invasive Surgery: Offering laparoscopic and robotic-assisted procedures for faster recovery times and reduced complications.

Women’s Health and Maternity Care: Comprehensive prenatal, labor and delivery, and postnatal services. Includes a state-of-the-art neonatal intensive care unit (NICU) for high-risk births.

Pediatrics: Expert care for children, including general pediatrics, pediatric surgery, and immunizations.

Facilities and Services

  • Advanced Surgical Unit with fully equipped operating rooms
  • Maternity Care Center with private birthing suites and family support services
  • Pediatric Care Unit with play therapy and family-centered approaches
  • Full-service Pharmacy and Outpatient Clinics for follow-up care
