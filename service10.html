Geriatric Care

Comprehensive Geriatric Care at LifeBridge Hospital

At LifeBridge Hospital, our geriatric care services are specially designed to address the unique health needs of older adults. Our compassionate team of geriatric specialists provides personalized, patient-centered care that promotes healthy aging and enhances quality of life.

Our expert team is experienced in managing common age-related conditions like diabetes, hypertension, arthritis, and heart disease. We offer comprehensive health assessments, creating individualized care plans that address mobility, memory concerns, and overall physical and emotional well-being. Our services range from routine screenings and chronic disease management to fall prevention, pain management, and palliative care.

With a collaborative, multidisciplinary approach, our geriatric care program unites geriatricians, nurses, physical therapists, and social workers to provide continuous, comprehensive support. We prioritize patient and family involvement, working closely with caregivers to educate and empower families, ensuring that each elderly patient receives the best possible care and support for living an independent, fulfilling life.

Meet Our Geriatric Care Team

At LifeBridge Hospital, our geriatric care team is made up of highly skilled and compassionate professionals dedicated to supporting the unique healthcare needs of older adults. Our multidisciplinary team includes experienced geriatricians, nurses, physical therapists, social workers, and nutritionists, all working collaboratively to provide holistic care. Every team member is committed to delivering personalized treatment plans that address the physical, mental, and emotional well-being of our elderly patients. By combining their expertise and working closely with families and caregivers, our team ensures that each patient receives attentive, comprehensive care aimed at promoting independence and enhancing quality of life.
