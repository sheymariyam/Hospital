Cardiology Services

Comprehensive Cardiology Services at LifeBridge Hospital

At LifeBridge Hospital, our cardiology department is dedicated to the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of heart-related conditions. As heart disease remains a leading cause of illness worldwide, we are committed to providing expert cardiovascular care through cutting-edge technology, specialized treatments, and a compassionate, patient-centered approach. Our cardiology team is highly skilled in managing a wide range of conditions, from hypertension and arrhythmias to complex heart failure and coronary artery disease.

Advanced Cardiac Diagnostic and Treatment Options

Our treatment options include both non-invasive and interventional therapies, tailored to each patient’s unique condition. These include:

  • Coronary Angioplasty and Stenting: A minimally invasive procedure to open narrowed or blocked coronary arteries, improving blood flow to the heart.
  • Ablation Therapy: A treatment for arrhythmias that uses heat or cold energy to correct abnormal heart rhythms.
  • Cardiac Rehabilitation: A comprehensive, medically supervised program designed to improve heart health following surgery or a cardiac event.
  • Heart Failure Management: A multidisciplinary approach to managing heart failure, including lifestyle modifications, medication therapy, and advanced treatments like implantable devices.
  • Valve Repair and Replacement: Expertise in both traditional and minimally invasive procedures to treat heart valve diseases.

Holistic and Collaborative Care

At LifeBridge Hospital, we take a holistic approach to cardiovascular care, focusing on prevention, early detection, and long-term management. Our team of cardiologists, interventional cardiologists, surgeons, nurses, and rehabilitation specialists work closely together to provide integrated care at every stage of the patient’s journey. We also emphasize patient education, equipping individuals and their families with the knowledge they need to manage their heart health effectively.

Symptoms of Heart Disease

Common symptoms of heart disease include chest pain, shortness of breath, palpitations, dizziness, and fatigue. However, symptoms may vary depending on the specific condition. Patients experiencing these symptoms should seek medical attention promptly, as early diagnosis and intervention are crucial to successful treatment outcomes.

Success Story: A New Lease on Life After Heart Surgery

One of our most inspiring success stories comes from George, a patient in his late 60s who was experiencing debilitating symptoms of heart disease. After being referred to LifeBridge Hospital, George underwent a comprehensive cardiac evaluation and was diagnosed with severe coronary artery disease. Our cardiology team immediately created a personalized treatment plan, including coronary angioplasty and stent placement to restore blood flow to his heart. Following his procedure, George enrolled in our cardiac rehabilitation program, where he received exercise therapy, nutritional counseling, and emotional support to help him regain his strength. Today, George has returned to his active lifestyle and reports feeling better than he has in years. "LifeBridge Hospital gave me my life back," George says. "Their care was thorough, compassionate, and tailored to my needs every step of the way."
