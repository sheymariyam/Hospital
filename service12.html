Oncology Services

Comprehensive Oncology Services at LifeBridge Hospital

At LifeBridge Hospital, our oncology services are dedicated to the diagnosis, treatment, and management of cancer, offering comprehensive care to patients at every stage of their journey. Oncology is the branch of medicine that deals with the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of cancer, a disease caused by the uncontrolled growth and spread of abnormal cells. Our experienced oncology team is equipped with the latest technology and treatments to provide personalized, compassionate care, focusing on both physical recovery and emotional well-being.

Symptoms of Cancer

Cancer symptoms can vary greatly depending on the type of cancer, but common symptoms include unexplained weight loss, fatigue, persistent pain, changes in skin appearance, and the presence of lumps or abnormal masses. Other symptoms may include difficulty swallowing, persistent cough, changes in bowel or bladder habits, and unexplained bleeding or bruising. It is important to consult a healthcare provider if any of these symptoms persist, as early detection and diagnosis are critical to effective treatment.

Holistic and Multidisciplinary Approach

Cancer treatment often requires a team approach, and at LifeBridge Hospital, we bring together oncologists, radiologists, surgeons, nurses, and other healthcare professionals to ensure comprehensive care. We take a holistic approach, offering not only medical treatments but also emotional and psychological support through counseling and support groups. Our goal is to provide each patient with the resources and care they need to face their diagnosis with confidence and strength.

Success Story: Overcoming Cancer with Comprehensive Care

At LifeBridge Hospital, every cancer journey is unique, and one of our most inspiring success stories is that of Jane, a breast cancer survivor. Diagnosed at an early stage, Jane was referred to our oncology team, where she received a comprehensive treatment plan that included surgery, followed by chemotherapy and radiation. Throughout her treatment, Jane received not only expert medical care but also emotional support through our counseling and patient support services. Today, Jane is cancer-free and credits the multidisciplinary team at LifeBridge Hospital for her recovery. "The care I received was phenomenal," Jane says. "The doctors, nurses, and staff were with me every step of the way, ensuring I understood my options and felt supported throughout the process. I truly felt like I was part of a family at LifeBridge."
